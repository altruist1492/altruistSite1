Pumpkin Pie (Yields 7 Pies)

Ingredients

  • 10 eggs
  • 2 cups light brown sugar
  • 2 cups dark brown sugar
  • 5 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tsp ginger
  • 2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp allspice
  • 2-1/2 tsp salt
  • 10 cups pureed pumpkin or winter squash
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 quart milk

  2. In a large bowl, beat the eggs until smooth.
  3. Add the remaining ingredients in the order given and mix until blended.
  4. Pour the mixture into prepared pie crusts.
  5. Bake at 425째F for 15 minutes, then reduce the heat to 345째F and continue baking for 1 hour, or until set.
  6. Allow to cool before serving.

Notes

The batter will be very runny until baked. When done baking, the centers will still be slightly wiggly but not soupy. After cooling, the pies will firm up and slices will retain their shape.

Serve with a dollop of whipped cream for extra indulgence.
